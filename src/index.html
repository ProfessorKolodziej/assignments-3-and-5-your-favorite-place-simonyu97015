Welcome to Ginzan Onsen

Nestled deep within the mountains of Japan lies the tranquil village of Ginzan Onsen, a place where time seems to stand still and the essence of traditional Japanese culture thrives. With its picturesque cobblestone streets, charming wooden ryokans (traditional inns), and soothing hot springs, Ginzan Onsen offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of modern life.

Top Attractions

  1. Ginzan Onsen Historic District
  2. Shirogane Falls
  3. Kogetsu Shuzo Sake Brewery
  4. Obanazawa Mine Okama Crater

Tips for Travelers

  • Remember to bring cash, as some establishments may not accept credit cards.
  • Respect local customs, such as removing your shoes before entering a ryokan.
  • Try traditional Japanese cuisine, such as sushi and tempura, at local restaurants.
  • Learn a few basic Japanese phrases to enhance your travel experience.
